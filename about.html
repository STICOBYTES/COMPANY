About Sticobytes Nig, Ltd

Sticobytes Nig, Ltd is an IT agency that was founded in the year 2020 and registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) in the year 2023 with Company Registration number 6942697. Our core specialties include:

  • Graphic Design
  • Web Design and Development
  • Mentorship

We also provide outdoor catering services and other related services to our esteemed clients.

At Sticobytes Nig, Ltd, we are committed to delivering high-quality services to our clients, using the latest technology and best practices in the industry. Our team of experts is dedicated to meeting and exceeding our clients' expectations, through innovative solutions and excellent customer service.
